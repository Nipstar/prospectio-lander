The problem

Finding owners by hand is slow, manual work.

Tracking down HMO landlords means hours across council planning portals, EPC searches and licence registers — and then the harder job of working out who actually owns each property and how to reach them.

Scattered sources

Every council runs its own portal. EPC and HMO registers sit elsewhere again. The data never lives in one place.

Ownership is hidden

A planning record rarely names the owner. Resolving the person or company behind a property takes more digging still.

Hours, every week

By the time a list is built and priced, the work has eaten a morning — and a competitor may already have written.

≈7hours a week, back
We built this for a Hampshire letting agent. It replaced about seven hours a week of manual research.
What it does

Six jobs, done quietly in the background.

From a public record to a priced lead with a name attached — ready for you to approve and post.

Public-data discovery

Prospectio continuously reads public planning, EPC and HMO-register data across councils, alongside trusted commercial property sources, surfacing opportunities worth a closer look.

Instant HMO appraisal

Every lead is priced the moment it appears — estimated valuation, room-by-room rents and projected gross yield — so you know what's worth pursuing before you pick up the phone.

Owner identification

Resolves the person or company behind each property — corporate or individual — and the best address to reach them, so you contact the decision-maker, not a managing agent.

Built-in CRM pipeline

A simple pipeline moves each owner from found to contacted to won, so nothing slips and you can see your whole patch at a glance.

Direct-mail outreach

Reaches owners by post with ready-made templates, with email as an option for corporate contacts. You review each send; Prospectio handles the rest.

Compliance handled

Suppression and opt-out are enforced before any send, the Mailing Preference Service is honoured, and each client's contact records stay isolated.

FAQ

The questions a careful agent asks first.

Is this legal and GDPR-compliant?
Yes. Outreach is postal-first under legitimate interest, with suppression and opt-out enforced. We honour the Mailing Preference Service and any do-not-contact request, and each client controls their own contact records.
How do you get the data?
From public registers — council planning applications, EPC records and HMO licence registers — combined with licensed commercial property data. We don't scrape private accounts or buy lists of dubious provenance.
Do I need technical skills to use it?
No. Prospectio is run for you. You tell us the areas and the kind of opportunity you want; it finds and prices them and prepares the outreach. You review and approve.
What does it cost?
Pricing is set per client, based on your area, volume and channels. That's why the only step here is a short call — we'll talk through what you need and quote it plainly. No hidden tiers.
Is my data shared with other users?
No. Each client is isolated. Your leads, your contacts and your outreach history are yours alone and are never pooled or resold.
